Priority Engine
Tells your team exactly which categories, brands, and SKUs to enrich first — and why
Search Priorities
Identifies categories and brands with the worst search visibility scores. Surfaces missing titles, descriptions, and structured data gaps that are suppressing Google indexing.
Source: GSC + OroCommerce search logs
Find Priorities
Surfaces products that appear in search but can't be filtered or compared on-site due to missing attributes. High bounce rate + low attribute completeness = Find priority.
Source: Akeneo completeness + GA4 bounce data
Buy Priorities
Identifies high-traffic products that aren't converting or have elevated return rates. Missing certifications, compatibility data, or specs are the most common causes.
Source: OroCommerce RFQ, orders & return data
How it works: Your catalog owner reviews the Priority Engine and adds items to the enrichment queue with one click. Pivotree's team processes the queue — normalizing, enriching, and QA-ing every SKU. Enriched data is returned to Akeneo for client review, then published to the OroCommerce eCommerce channel. Scores update within 48–72 hours of publish.
